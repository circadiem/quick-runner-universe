A continuity tracker for stories told in the Quick Runner Universe. Updated as new stories are created.

## Canon Events (Chronological)

### Pre-Origin
- Quick Runner is an ordinary kid who loves running and his dog
- They race through Meadowbrook Park every day after school

### The Origin Storm
- A strange electric-blue storm hits the city
- The kid saves a small bird from a falling branch
- The world slows — raindrops hang in the air
- The wind whispers: "You were brave. Now you're fast."
- Both the kid and Wonder Dog receive powers
- **Source:** Origin Story Script (24 panels)

### Post-Origin
- Quick Runner discovers his powers
- The Speed Lab is established beneath his house
- First encounters with Captain Clumsy *(not yet scripted)*
- First encounter with The Shadow Thorn *(not yet scripted)*
