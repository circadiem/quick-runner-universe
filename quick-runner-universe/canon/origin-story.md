# Quick Runner: Origin — The Storm That Made a Hero

> 24-panel comic script, 6 pages. Full narrative arc.

---

## Summary

A kid and his dog are racing through Meadowbrook Park when a strange, electric-blue storm rolls in. While everyone else runs for cover, the kid spots a small bird frozen with fear beneath a cracking tree branch. He doesn't think about it — he runs. The world slows. The rain hangs in the air. He saves the bird. And when the storm passes, he discovers he's become something more.

## Pages

- **Page 1: "A Normal Day"** — Establishing the kid and his dog, their daily race through the park
- **Page 2: "The Storm Rolls In"** — The sky changes, the crowd panics, the kid and dog stand still
- **Page 3: "The Moment of Courage"** — The cracking branch, the terrified bird, the decision
- **Page 4: "The Rescue"** — Time slows, the run, the save, the crash
- **Page 5: "The Awakening"** — Blue sparks, the first super step, the first super run
- **Page 6: "The Promise"** — The whisper in the wind, the costume reveal, the running shadow

## Key Beats

- The kid doesn't choose to be a hero — he just sees someone who needs help and runs
- Wonder Dog stays by his side through the entire storm, earning his own powers through loyalty
- The wind whispers: "You were brave. Now you're fast."
- Final panel: a small sparrow watches from a branch as the kid walks home — the bird he saved, watching over its hero
- Panel 22 foreshadow: the boy's shadow on the ground is running, even though the boy is walking — a hint of both his destiny and the Shadow Thorn
